## Knowledge base snapshot
Acme Cloud offers three tiers: Starter, Team, and Enterprise. Starter includes 5 seats and 10GB storage and a 48h support SLA. Team includes 25 seats, 100GB storage, and a 24h SLA. Enterprise is custom-priced with a 4h SLA, SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated success manager. The dashboard lives at app.acmecloud.example. Billing is monthly or annual, and annual billing carries a 15% discount applied on the next cycle with proration. The S3 integration requires an IAM role granting s3:GetObject and a bucket policy that allows the Acme principal; cross-region buckets require explicit enablement under Settings > Integrations > Region. SSO is Okta and Azure AD via SAML, configured under Security > SSO using the identity provider metadata URL. Rate limits are 60 requests/minute on Starter, 600 on Team, and custom on Enterprise. Data export is available as CSV or JSON from Settings > Data. Webhooks support retries with exponential backoff for up to 24 hours.
